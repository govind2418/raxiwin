How to win at each Raxi Game

Winning at Raxi Snake

Hug the edges early and leave the center open — it gives you room to turn without trapping your own tail as you grow.

Winning at Raxi 2048

Pick one corner and never move your highest tile away from it. Build descending rows toward that corner to keep merges predictable.

Winning at Memory Match

Only flip a second card once you've committed the first to memory. Scan the board in the same order every round to build a mental map.

Winning at Tic-Tac-Toe

Take the center on your first move when possible, then always block before you attack — a forced draw beats a rushed loss.

Winning at Whack-a-Mole

Keep your cursor near the middle of the board between hits so every hole is roughly the same reach.

Winning at Reaction Test

Rest your finger just above the click target and focus on color, not motion — anticipating the flash only costs you false starts.

Raxi Win reflects only your local browser progress — no money, deposits, or prizes are involved. Clearing your browser data will reset these scores.
