Raxi Login

Raxi Login — a lightweight profile, not a password vault

Raxi Game doesn't need your email or a password to personalize your experience. Pick a display name below and it's saved on this device, so your scores on Raxi Win are labeled as yours.

Your display name is stored only in this browser (localStorage). Nothing is sent to a server, and no personal information is collected. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Why Raxi Login works this way

Most games ask for an email and password before you've even decided if you like them. Raxi Game flips that: play first from the Games hub, and only set up a profile if you want your best scores clearly labeled. It's login without the friction — and without collecting data we don't need.
