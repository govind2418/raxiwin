Rules & Controls

How to play every game on Raxi Game

Quick-reference rules and controls for all six titles. Every game opens instantly from the Games hub — no tutorial screens, no forced onboarding.

Raxi Snake

Goal: Eat orbs to grow as long as possible without hitting a wall or your own tail.

Controls: Arrow keys or WASD to steer. Each orb eaten adds one point.

Ends when: The snake's head hits a wall or its own body.

Raxi 2048

Goal: Slide numbered tiles to merge matching values and reach the 2048 tile.

Controls: Arrow keys move every tile in that direction; equal tiles merge into one.

Ends when: The 4x4 board is full and no merges are possible.

Memory Match

Goal: Clear the board in as few moves as possible by matching all 6 pairs.

Controls: Click a card to flip it, then click a second — matching pairs stay face up.

Ends when: All pairs are matched; your move count becomes your score.

Tic-Tac-Toe

Goal: Get three of your marks in a row before the Raxi CPU does.

Controls: Click any empty cell to place your X; the CPU responds automatically.

Ends when: Three in a row is achieved, or the board fills for a draw.

Whack-a-Mole

Goal: Score as many hits as possible in 30 seconds.

Controls: Click the hole the instant it lights up.

Ends when: The 30-second timer runs out.

Reaction Test

Goal: Record the fastest reaction time in milliseconds.

Controls: Click to arm the test, wait for the box to turn green, then click again immediately.

Ends when: Each round completes in one click — try again to beat your time.
