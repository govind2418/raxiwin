Frequently Asked Questions

Raxi Game FAQ

Direct answers about games, Raxi Win, Raxi Login and the Raxi App.

What is Raxi Game?

Raxi Game is a free browser gaming site with six skill-based games — Snake, 2048, Memory Match, Tic-Tac-Toe, Whack-a-Mole and a Reaction Test. Every game runs instantly in your browser with no downloads or installs required.

Is Raxi Game free to play?

Yes. All Raxi Game titles are completely free, with no purchases, subscriptions, or real-money wagering of any kind.

Does Raxi Win involve real money?

No. Raxi Win tracks your personal best scores, stored locally in your browser, and awards skill-based achievement badges. There is no cash prize pool, deposit, or wagering involved.

How does Raxi Login work?

Raxi Login lets you set a display name that is saved on your device so your scores on Raxi Win are labeled as yours. No email, password, or personal data is required or collected.

How do I install the Raxi App?

On Android or desktop Chrome, tap "Install Raxi App" on the Raxi App page or use your browser's "Install app" option. On iPhone or iPad, open the site in Safari, tap Share, then "Add to Home Screen".

Do I need to create an account to play?

No. You can open any game from the Games hub and start playing immediately. Logging in is optional and only used to label your local scores.

Which games can I play on Raxi Game?

Six games are currently available: Raxi Snake, Raxi 2048, Memory Match, Tic-Tac-Toe, Whack-a-Mole, and a Reaction Test, all playable from the Games hub.

Is my data safe on Raxi Game?

Yes. Raxi Game does not collect personal data. Scores and profile names are stored only in your browser's local storage and are never transmitted to a server. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
