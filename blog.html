Why the Raxi App skips the app store

Every native app update means a store review queue, forced permissions, and megabytes of download. The Raxi App is a Progressive Web App instead: install it straight from rexigame.com in seconds, and every update is live the moment you reopen it — no waiting on approval, no bloated download.

Jun 18, 2026

Inside Raxi Win: how local leaderboards work

Raxi Win doesn't run on a server — your best scores live in your browser's localStorage, which is why they load instantly and never depend on our uptime. Log in on Raxi Login to label them with a display name, and they'll stay put until you clear your browser data.

5 ways to push your Raxi Snake high score higher

Small habits separate a score of 8 from a score of 30: steer in wide loops instead of tight turns, keep the center of the board clear as long as possible, and commit to a direction before you need to react. Full breakdown on the Raxi Win strategy page.
