Privacy Policy & Terms of Use

Last updated: July 10, 2026. Raxi Game is built to collect as little as possible — here's exactly what happens with your data, and the rules for using the site.

Privacy Policy

What we collect

Raxi Game does not require an account, email address, or password to play. When you use Raxi Login, the display name you choose is stored only in your browser's localStorage — it is never sent to a server or shared with anyone.

Game scores

Your best scores on each game, shown on Raxi Win, are calculated and stored entirely in your browser. We have no server-side database of player scores.

Cookies & tracking

Raxi Game does not use advertising or tracking cookies. Any analytics we add in the future will be limited to anonymous, aggregate traffic statistics and will be disclosed here.

Third-party links

Some pages link to outside resources, including occasional affiliate links to relevant products. Clicking an outbound link takes you to a third-party site with its own privacy practices, which we don't control.

Children's privacy

Raxi Game does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children, because we don't collect personal information at all.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the date above and the content on this page.

Terms of Use

Using Raxi Game

Raxi Game and every game on it are provided free of charge for personal, non-commercial entertainment. No purchase, deposit, or payment is required or accepted, and the site does not offer real-money gambling, betting, or wagering products of any kind.

No warranty

Raxi Game is provided "as is." Scores stored in your browser can be lost if you clear site data, switch browsers, or switch devices — we recommend not relying on them for anything beyond casual tracking.

Acceptable use

Don't attempt to disrupt, reverse-engineer for malicious purposes, or abuse Raxi Game's games or infrastructure. Content on this site is for personal use; republishing it elsewhere requires permission.

Changes to the service

We may add, change, or remove games and features at any time without notice.

Contact

Questions about these terms or our privacy practices can be sent via the Contact page or to support@rexigame.com.
