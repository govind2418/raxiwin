About Us

Raxi Game is a free browser gaming arena

Raxi Game exists to answer one question: how good can free, instant, browser-only games feel? No installs, no accounts required to start, no real-money wagering — just fast games you can open in one tab and close whenever you want.

What we build

Small, sharp browser games — Snake, 2048, Memory Match, Tic-Tac-Toe, Whack-a-Mole, and a Reaction Test — built in lightweight JavaScript so they load fast on any connection or device.

What we don't do

Raxi Game never asks for payment, never runs real-money wagering or betting, and never sells your gameplay data. Scores stay on your device unless you choose to log in.

Why "Raxi"

Raxi is our take on a competitive arena: dark, focused, no distractions. It's the name behind every game, every leaderboard entry on Raxi Win, and the Raxi App.

How Raxi Game works

Open any game from the Games hub and start playing immediately — nothing to download or install. Your best score for each game is saved locally in your browser. Create a free account on Raxi Login if you want that progress to persist across visits, or install the Raxi App to launch games from your home screen like a native app.

Our commitment

We built Raxi Game around three rules: every game stays free, every game stays skill-based rather than chance-based wagering, and every design choice favors speed and clarity over clutter. That's the whole pitch.
